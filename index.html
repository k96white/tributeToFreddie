Frederick Mercury a.k.a (Freddie Mercury) one of the greatest singer of all time was born on 5 September 1946. He is the lead vocalist of Queen Band. He was named as Farrokh Bulsara by his parents.He was known for his flamboyant stage persona and four-octave vocal range. He is known for writing & singing greatest hits like Bohemian Rhapsody,We are the Champions, Killer Queen,The Love of My Life ,SomeBody To Love,Crazy Little Thing Called Love,Don't Stop Me Now and many more.
